Big Mac®

580 Cal.

When a craving hits, it’s easy to stop thinking and simply take a bite. The Big Mac® is built on layers — much like us. At first glance, it looks balanced, carefully put together, almost perfect.

But beneath the surface, each layer tells a different story. Choices made for convenience, comfort chosen over better judgment, and small decisions repeated until they become habits. It’s familiar, easy, and hard to resist — even when we know better.

Like many things in life, it satisfies in the moment. But what it represents lasts much longer — a reminder of how often we trade awareness for ease, and how those small compromises slowly build into something bigger.
