Big Mac®

580 Cal.

For a long time, I could not uncover the secrets of the titans moving around us. They were nothing like us and could do many things we couldn’t. Every few seconds, a titan would eat one of us. To us, it felt like they did it just for bloodlust. Researchers have examined them and concluded that they do not even need food to survive.

My life was short, and I had always wanted to see the outside world — beyond the walls that confined me. Big Macs had tried, but everything depended on luck. Some were taken outside, while others spent their entire lives trapped within the walls.

No one knew what truly happened to those who were taken, but one thing was certain: death followed.

When I was 11 minutes old, a titan approached me and picked me up, holding me in its hands. I knew it was the end of my life. I recalled every memory and how peaceful everything once was.

However, just as I was about to die, I felt a surge of energy run through me. My structure broke apart — layers separated, stretched, and reformed. When I looked again, I saw a titan standing where I once had been.

I had become a titan.

Suddenly, I could move. A flood of memories overwhelmed me, and I understood everything. I was part of the royal family, and my father had also been a titan who passed this power to me.

The reason I could not remember any of this before was because my father had removed part of my memory — my lettuce — from me. Now, at the edge of my life, I had regained those memories.

The titan that tried to kill me was now nothing more than a flattened shape beneath me.

And for the first time… I had a name.

Case Oh.
