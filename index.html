Big Mac®

580 Cal.

When a craving hits for those two all-beef patties, it’s time to stop thinking about what’s on a Big Mac® and take a bite. The Big Mac is a 100% beef burger with a taste like no other. The mouthwatering perfection starts with two patties and Big Mac sauce sandwiched between a sesame seed bun.

It’s topped off with pickles, crisp shredded lettuce, finely chopped onion and a slice of cheese. It contains no artificial flavors, preservatives, or added colors from artificial sources. Our pickle contains an artificial preservative, so skip it if you like.

There are 580 calories in a Big Mac from McDonald’s. Pair it with any of our beverages or grab a Big Mac Combo Meal with our World Famous Fries® and Coca-Cola® or any of your favorite fountain drinks.
