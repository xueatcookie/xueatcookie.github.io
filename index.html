For a long time, I could not uncover the secrets of the titans moving around us, they were nothing shaped like us and could do many things we couldn’t. Every few seconds, there will be a titan eating us up, to us, they did it just for bloodlust. Researchers have examined them and concluded that they do not need food to survive. My life was short and I had always wanted to see the outside world; outside of the walls which confined me. Big Macs have tried but everything is based on luck, some Big Macs are brought outside, but others spent their whole lives inside the walls. No one knows what happened to the Big Macs that were brought out by titans, but death was certain.

When I was 11minutes old, a titan approached me and picked me up, then it held me in its hands. I knew it was the end of my life. I recalled every memory and how peaceful it was. However, just as I was about to die, I felt a surge of energy run through me and I broke apart, layers separated, stretched, and reformed me. I checked myself, only to see a titan at the exact spot I was in. I had become a titan.

I could suddenly move, and a surge of memories overwhelmed me. I understood everything. I was part of the royal family, and my father was also a titan who had passed me this power. The reason I could not remember those things is because my father had removed part of my memory, my lettuce from me. Now that I am about to die, I inherited the titan form and could remember those memories again. The other titan who tried to kill me was squashed into pancake under my weight. I Finally had a name – Case Oh.
